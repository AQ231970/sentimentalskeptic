A NOVICE WRITER'S BLOG

aysha a.q.

Welcome and thank you for paying a visit to my personal website and literary blog. As a lover and explorer of literature, history and epistemology, I sometimes become overwhelmed into an intellectual haze by the facts, ideas, and stories I encounter. Within documenting the lessons learned and expanding on my own thoughts in this little corner of the internet is perhaps a small remedy.

This blog is also a way for me to practice my writing and record my progress, while exploring and exercising my creative faculties.
